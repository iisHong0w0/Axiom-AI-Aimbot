怎麼啟動?
	先安裝python-3.11.0-amd64.exe
	然後打開"啟動Launcher.bat"

為什麼安裝了python後還是啟動不了?
	安裝python時記得勾選"Add python.exe to PATH"

How to start?
	First, install python-3.11.0-amd64.exe.
	Then open "Launcher.bat".
 
Why can’t it start even after installing Python?
	When installing Python, make sure to check the option “Add python.exe to PATH.”
